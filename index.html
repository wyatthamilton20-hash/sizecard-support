Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a family member?

Tap the "Add Family Member" button on your home screen, then enter their information and sizing details.

How do I share my sizing card?

Tap the share button on any sizing card to generate a unique link you can send via text, email, or any messaging app.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your sizing information is stored securely and encrypted. You control what you share and with whom.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
